## Fine Dining

### 1. Ministry of Crab
- **Location:** Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct, Colombo 01
- **Cuisine:** Seafood, Sri Lankan
- **Price Range:** LKR 5,000 – 20,000 per person
- **Hours:** 12PM–11PM daily
- **Features:** Asia's 50 Best Restaurants list, open kitchen, reservations recommended
- **Signature Dishes:**
  - Pepper Crab – LKR 5,000–8,000
  - Garlic Chili Crab – LKR 5,500–9,000
  - King Prawns in Curry Sauce – LKR 4,500–7,500
- **User Reviews:**
  - "Sri Lanka's most iconic restaurant. The crab is legendary." – TripAdvisor (4.5/5, 2,500+ reviews)
  - "Worth the splurge. Fresh lagoon crabs cooked to perfection." – Google
  - "Book ahead — walk-ins often wait 1+ hours." – Google
- **Sources:** Menueka.com, TripAdvisor, Google Reviews, Wanderlog

### 2. Nuga Gama – Cinnamon Grand
- **Location:** Cinnamon Grand Hotel, Colombo 03
- **Cuisine:** Sri Lankan (Traditional Village Experience)
- **Price Range:** LKR 3,500 – 6,000 per person
- **Hours:** Dinner daily
- **Features:** 200-year-old banyan tree setting, thatched huts, clay pot dining, buffet style
- **Signature Dishes:**
  - Traditional Rice & Curry Buffet – LKR 3,500–5,000
  - Hopper Station (egg hoppers, string hoppers) – included in buffet
  - Coconut Sambol & Spicy Chutneys – included
- **User Reviews:**
  - "A village in the city — immersive and delicious." – Google (4.3/5)
  - "Best place to try authentic Sri Lankan for the first time." – TripAdvisor
- **Sources:** Menueka.com, VisitSriLanka.ai

## Cost Context (2025–2026)

- **Cheap restaurant meal:** ~LKR 1,000 (~$3.1 USD)
- **3-course meal for two (mid-range):** ~LKR 7,000 (~$22 USD)
- **Fast food combo (McDonald's etc.):** ~LKR 2,400 (~$7.5 USD)
- **Local beer (0.5L draught):** ~LKR 600 (~$1.9 USD)
- **Imported beer (0.33L):** ~LKR 900 (~$2.8 USD)
- **Cappuccino:** ~LKR 710 (~$2.2 USD)
- **Coke/Pepsi (0.33L):** ~LKR 205 (~$0.64 USD)
- Colombo commands the highest food prices in Sri Lanka — 20–40% more than Kandy/Jaffna
- Street food at Pettah Market: hoppers LKR 150–300, isso wade LKR 100–400
- Tourist area premium: 300–500% markup vs local "hotels"
