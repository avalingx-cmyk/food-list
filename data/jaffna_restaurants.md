## Research Notes
- Jaffna is renowned for its unique Tamil-influenced cuisine, especially **Jaffna Crab Curry**, **Jaffna Mutton Curry**, **Kool** (spicy seafood soup), and **Palmyrah**-based dishes.
- **Vegetarian options** are abundant and often served on banana leaves in traditional eateries.
- Prices are generally lower than Colombo; a satisfying local meal can cost LKR 200–500, while mid-range dining runs LKR 600–1,500.
- Many small restaurants operate limited lunch menus (rice & curry only) and expand to full menus (dosas, kottu, parotta) after 6:00 PM.
- Jaffna cuisine uses more red chili, black pepper, and curry leaves than Colombo — significantly spicier.
